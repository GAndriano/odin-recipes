Margherita Pizza

Pizza is one of the world's most reknowned foods. While created in Italy, America has adopted it into its' own cultural staple. It is a delicious combination of bread, cheese, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag pizza dough
  • 16 oz pizza sauce
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp pecorino romano cheese
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 roll of unsalted mozzarella, sliced
  • 2 tbsp basil

Steps

  1. Let dough rise by leaving it out on a counter for 30 mins.
    2.  
  2. Stretch the dough. Place pre-made dough on top of flour. Stretch the dough by pinching the outside in a circular motion to spread it out. Then alternate between pinching and pulling the dough from underneath with knuckles in an outward motion. When it's large enough, transfer it to a sheet pan lined with parchment paper and al ight drizzle of olive oil.
    3.  
  3. Bake the dough in the oven at 450 for 7 minutes.
    4.  
  4. Once the dough is cooked, take it out and preheat the oven to 500. Brush olive oil around the permieter of the dough to create a golden crust. Add the sauce, cheeses, and basil.
    5.  
  5. Bake the pizza at 500 for 7-10 minutes, removing when the mozzarella cheese has melted. Allow the pizza to cool for a few minutes before serving.
    6.  
  6. Enjoy!
